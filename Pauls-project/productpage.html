Flex E-Pen (Prototype Model)

Our Flex E-Pen stands out from it's competitors by offering the ability to function both digitally and non-digitally.
The non-digital aspect of our design replicates a typical ballpoint pen. However, you can also replace the ink using an ink piston fitted inside.
Additionally, the pen comes with various interchangable nips so you can choose what's right for you.

The pen's ergonomic design is suited for user's intending to use the device for extended periods of time such as artists, scholars and anyone inbetween.
